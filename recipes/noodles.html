Noodles

Description

Noodles are long, thin strands made from dough, usually prepared from wheat, rice, or other grains.

They can be served in soups, stir-fries, or with different sauces and toppings.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Optional
  • Eggs
  • Kansui
  • Alternative starches

Step-by-step guide

  1. Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and a half-tablespoon of oil to the water.
  3. Drop in the noodles. Cook until they are soft but still retain a slight bite.
  4. Drain the noodles immediately using a colander or strainer.
  5. Rinse them thoroughly under cold running water and then Shake off as much excess water as possible.
  6. Transfer the drained noodles to a bowl or pan and drizzle with 1 to 2 teaspoons of vegetable or toasted sesame oil.
