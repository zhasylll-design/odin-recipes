Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a baked Italian dish made with layers of pasta, sauce, cheese, and sometimes meat or vegetables.

It is known for its rich flavor and hearty texture.

Ingredients

  • 9-12 sheets of Lasagna noodles
  • 1-1.5 lbs of ground beef and italian sausage
  • Tomatoes
  • Aromatics and Veggies
  • Seasoning(salt, black pepper, Italian seasoning)
  • Olive oil
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Step-by-step guide

  1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, cook 1 lb ground beef and 1 lb ground Italian sausage
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, garlic, and your favorite Italian herbs. Let it simmer on low for 15–20 minutes so the flavors meld and the sauce thickens.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese , grated Parmesan, eggs, salt, and pepper. The eggs help bind the mixture so it doesn't run when you cut into it.
  4. Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente (tender but still firm). Drain, rinse with cold water, and lay them flat on parchment paper to prevent sticking.
  5. Layer with pattern of : Base(meat sauce), Pasta, Cheese, Meat and Mozzarella, Repeat
  6. Preheat oven to 190°C and bake for 40 minutes
