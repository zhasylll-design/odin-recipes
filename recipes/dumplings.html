Dumplings

Description

  • Dumpling or gyoza wrappers
  • Ground pork
  • Napa cabbage
  • Green onions and ginger
  • Soy sauce and sesame oil for umami
  • Shaoxing wine
  • White pepper and salt
  1. Place a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat and add 1-2 tablespoons of neutral oil.

  2. Once the oil is hot, place the dumplings in the pan, flat-side down, making sure they don't touch.

  3. Fry for 1-2 minutes until the bottoms are lightly golden.

  4. Pour in about \(\frac{1}{3}\) to \(\frac{1}{2}\) cup of water, and immediately cover with a tight-fitting lid.

  5. Lower the heat to medium-low and let them steam for 6-8 minutes

  6. Once the water is completely evaporated, remove the lid.
